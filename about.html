My name is Stephanie Raymos.
I started using essential oils in High School when a friend gave me a mixture for a cold.
After experiencing the benefits within a few hours I decided to do a little research.
A little research turned into months of dedicated study with books and print outs and highlighters.
My collection grew over the years entering adulthood and now at 27 years old I have every oil offered by Doterra.
Anytime anything goes wrong, there's an oil for it. I have yet to find something this doesn't apply to.
Thank you for reading! I hope this has inspired you to give them a try.
